# Midnight Faucet

Midnight faucet is a tool provided for developers to acquire free testnet tokens (tDUST) to experiment with Midnight DApps on the testnet. 

[Link to related documentation](../develop/tutorial/using/faucet#get-some-tdust)

To download the component, click the appropriate link under **Artifacts**.
