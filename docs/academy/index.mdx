# Midnight Developer Academy

Welcome to the Midnight Developer Academy — your guided journey into blockchain development with a focus on zero-knowledge proofs, privacy, and the Midnight platform. 

Whether you're just getting started or looking to sharpen your skills, each module is designed to build your understanding step by step — from foundational concepts to building full DApps.

## What’s Next?

The Midnight Developer Academy is just getting started. Modules 4–8 will expand into advanced areas including:

- **Kachina Protocol** — the privacy-preserving smart contract layer
- **Developing on Midnight** — using Compact to build on Midnight
- **Building DApps on Midnight** — building decentralized applications on Midnight
- **Security & Best Practices** — secure coding and privacy decisions
- **Capstone Project** — submit your DApp to a Midnight Hackathon and earn a Certificate of Completion NFT

These modules are scheduled for release between **May and June 2025**, with continuous updates based on community feedback. Stay tuned for hands-on activities, developer tracks, and advanced zero-knowledge tooling.
